More About me

Design is another passion of mine. I am eager to pursue roles that allow me to merge my prior experience with creative development. I thrive in fast-paced environments, enjoy tackling problem-solving challenges, and love working collaboratively with diverse teams.
Lastly, a fun fact is that I love music/to dance in all styles! Hip-hop, jazz, and contemporary just to name a few.
